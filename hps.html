Southampton University Human Powered Submarine

I am Engineering Officer for the SUHPS team. It is my job to oversee the engineering sub-teams and take a project management role, ensuring design and manufacturing runs smoothly and we meet all competition deadlines. I also organise and oversee diver and pilot training, providing training routines and liaising with Andark, a local diving centre who provide diver training and equipment for the team.

I am also in charge of media relations, talking to the press, and building and maintaining the SUHPS website.
